Our Five Stage Laundry Process

  • Sorting

  • This is the first stage in the laundry process. Proper sorting is crucial to avoid re-washing items or damaging them. Sort your laundry by color, fabric type, and level of soiling.

  • Pre-Treatment

  • The second stage involves treating stains before washing the laundry. Apply stain remover directly to the stained area. Use detergent as a pre-treatment. White Vinegar or Baking Soda can also help with stain removal.

  • Washing

  • The third stage is where laundry is actually cleaned. Use a gentle laundry detergent, wash delicate items in cool water, and avoid using too much detergent. Consider using a mesh laundry bag for extra protection.

  • Drying

  • The fourth stage involves drying the laundry. Hang clothes to dry naturally. Clothesline or Laundry Rack are Ideal for air drying. Use a dryer on a low heat setting. Items like jeans and sweaters can be hung up to dry instead of using the dryer.
